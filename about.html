Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Savanna Lingo Ltd was founded in 2018 with a simple yet powerful vision: to break down language barriers and help businesses communicate effectively in an increasingly connected world. What began as a small translation service has evolved into a comprehensive language and marketing solution provider.

Our founder, recognizing the growing need for culturally-sensitive content across global markets, assembled a team of linguistic and marketing experts united by their passion for effective cross-cultural communication.

Today, we serve clients across five continents, helping them connect with their audiences through precise translations, strategic social media campaigns, and engaging content creation. Our commitment to excellence and cultural understanding remains at the heart of everything we do.
